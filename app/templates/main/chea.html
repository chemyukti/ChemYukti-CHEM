Program Overview

CHEA is not a replacement for school or college. It is a carefully designed add-on programme that runs alongside whatever your child is already studying. Based on the CHEM Framework (Cybernetics & Human Intelligence Methods), it adds the one layer that formal education rarely provides: the ability to think well, adapt fast, and act effectively.
