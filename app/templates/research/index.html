CHEM Research

Cybernetics & Human Engineering Methods

Framework Overview

CHEM is built upon the principles of cybernetics—the science of communications and automatic control systems in both machines and living things. We apply these methods to human learning to create high-efficiency educational environments.

Key Research Areas

Investigating how feedback loops can be optimized to reduce cognitive load and increase retention in student-centered learning environments.

Developing methods to enhance neural plasticity and focus through structured learning routines and adaptive intelligence tests.
